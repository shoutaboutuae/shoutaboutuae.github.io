Attractions in Dubai:

  1. Burj Khalifa, the tallest building in the world

  2. Dubai Mall, the largest mall in the world currently

  3. Dubai Frame, It is a large rectangle that puts the Burj Khalifa in the middle so that you can take a picture

  4. Dubai Aquarium, located in Dubai Mall and offers a tour of the inside of the aquarium showing all the animals

  5. Burj Al Arab, one of the first 7 star hotels in the world

  6. Dubai Opera, shows many famous acts live in the opera in downtown Dubai

  7. Dubai Parks and Resorts, contains various theme parks including motiongate, bolywood parks, legoland, legoland water park and many more

  8. Aquaventure and Wild Wadi, both waterparks with various different rides located in Atlantis and Jumeirah Respectively

  9. Atlanits, a hotel featuring a multitude of resteraunts, an aquarium and a water park located on an island
