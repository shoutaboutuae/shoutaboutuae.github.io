About The UAE:
If you are reading this website chances are you have just recently moved to the UAE and want to know some basic information about this country. The UAE has 7 emirates, Dubai, Sharjah, Fujairah, Ajman, Umm Al Quwain, Ras Al Khaimah and the capital Abu Dhabi. The UAE has the tallest building in the world, Burj Khalifa which is in the heart of Dubai, and right next to it is the world's largest mall, the Dubai Mall.
